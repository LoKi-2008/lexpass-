🌍 LexPass

«Know your rights. Anywhere. Anytime.»

LexPass is an AI-powered global legal awareness platform designed to help people understand their rights, laws, and correct actions in real-life situations.

---

🚀 Vision

Most people don’t know their rights.

LexPass solves this by combining:

- ⚖️ Verified legal laws
- 🤖 AI-powered explanations
- 🎮 Daily learning system
- 🚨 Emergency legal guidance

---

🔥 Features

🤖 AI Legal Agent

- Gives rights based on real laws
- Shows exact legal sections
- Explains in simple language
- Provides actionable steps

---

🌍 Global Coverage

- Designed for all countries
- Structured law database

---

🎮 Learning System

- Duolingo-style learning
- Flashcards + scenarios

---

🚨 Emergency Mode

- Quick legal guidance
- Real-time help

---

🧠 Tech Stack

- FastAPI (Backend)
- Supabase (Database)
- Claude API (AI)
- Next.js (Frontend)
